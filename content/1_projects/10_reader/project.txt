Title: Reading Reader

----

Category: Book Design + Typography

----

Year: 2019

----

Link: 

----

What: The Reading Reader summarizes various texts by designer Jeffery Keedy. It is a whimsical rendition of his quotes, such as, "The first thing one learns about typography & type design is that these rules are made to be broken." His merciless thoughts on graphic design and typography are understandably humorous to designers and this book aims to offer a playful caricature.

----

Why: The book is set in <b>Helvetica Neue</b> and features an equally bold colour scheme. Since the quotes are anything but mundane, the Reading Reader is unapologetic in combining this visually striking combination.

----

How: The idea behind the Reading Reader sprung from reading a lot about design, written by designers. It posed an interesting challenge to try and visualize. It adheres to almost none of the rules of typography, whilst not being a chaotic mess.
