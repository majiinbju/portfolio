Title: East River Raiders

----

Category: Brand Design

----

Year: 2017

----

Link: 

----

What: East River Raiders is a conceptual project that draws inspiration from New York City. It is a fictional football (soccer) club based in the Bowery district. Drawing inspiration from the East River, it pays homage to the rich culture and heritage of the area.

----

Why: The brand design for the East River Raiders comprises an application, soccer jerseys, Celtic patterns and the logo. Right in the center is a Celtic knot, which alludes to the mass Irish immigration that the Bowery area witnessed during the turn of the 19th Century.

----

How: The colour scheme is a reference to New York's official colours. The blue is supplemented by Celtic patterns such as the knot. It is paying respects to the historic association the Bowery area holds with Irish culture.
