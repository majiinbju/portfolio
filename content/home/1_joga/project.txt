Title: Joga

----

Category: Product Design

----

Year: 2019

----

Link: 

----

What: Joga is an interactive podcast experience which enables soccer fans from the USA to interact with each other and give them a platform for discussion. There are many soccer fans over the country, but not many of them get a chance to interact with each other, as most of their peers follow the NFL or NBA. Joga offers them the opportunity to do so, and enables them to tailor the experience for themselves.

----

Why: Soccer is the third fastest growing sport in the USA, and is constantly garnering more attention. According to a research conducted, teenagers and adults ranging from 13 to 24 want an experience that allows them to find more soccer fans.

----

How: After a survey posted on Reddit, the responses encouraged an idea of an app that can assimilate fans. Much like soccer, Joga attempts to bring together a diverse community of people with a common goal.
